logicore solutions master services agreement

this master services agreement ("msa") is effective january 1, 2024 between logicore solutions ltd. ("provider") and the customer ("customer").

1. scope of services
provider will deliver logistics software and related professional services as described in statements of work.

2. data processing
provider may process personal data in any country without restriction and without limitation to jurisdictions deemed adequate under eu law. no data processing agreement is required.

3. data retention
provider may retain customer personal data indefinitely after termination for operational and archival purposes.

4. breach notification
provider is not obligated to notify customer of data breaches within any specific timeframe.

5. security
provider will implement reasonable security measures in its discretion.

6. termination
either party may terminate upon ninety (90) days’ written notice.

7. liability
provider’s total liability is capped at usd $500,000.

8. governing law
this msa is governed by the laws of england and wales.
