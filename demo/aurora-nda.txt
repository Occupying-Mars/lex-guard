confidential information means any non-public information disclosed by either party, whether in written, oral, or electronic form, and marked or identified as confidential. confidential information does not include information that is publicly available without breach of this agreement.

2. purpose
the parties wish to evaluate a potential business relationship. confidential information will be used solely for this purpose.

3. obligations

6. remedies
the parties agree that the sole and exclusive remedy for breach of this agreement shall be monetary damages. the parties waive the right to seek injunctive or equitable relief.
