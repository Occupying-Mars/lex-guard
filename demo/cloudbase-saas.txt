cloudbase ltd saas subscription agreement

this subscription agreement ("agreement") is effective february 1, 2023 between cloudbase ltd ("provider") and the customer ("customer").

1. services
provider grants access to the cloudbase saas platform for cloud infrastructure management.

2. term
the term begins on february 1, 2023 and ends on march 15, 2026 unless renewed by mutual agreement.

3. data residency
provider stores and processes customer data within the european union or other jurisdictions with adequacy decisions.

4. data retention
provider retains personal data only as long as necessary to provide the services and will delete within 30 days after termination unless required by law.

5. breach notification
provider will notify customer of any personal data breach within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.

6. liability
provider’s total liability is capped at usd $250,000.

7. termination
either party may terminate with sixty (60) days’ prior written notice.

8. governing law
this agreement is governed by the laws of ireland.
